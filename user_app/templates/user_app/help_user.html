Help & FAQ for JobSeeker

Q. How do I search for a job on JobPortal?

Use the search bar on the homepage or job listings page. You can filter jobs by title, location, or category.

Q. How can I apply for a job?

Once you find a job you like, click on it and then click the "Apply" button. Make sure you are logged in and your resume is uploaded.

Q. Do I need to create an account to apply?

Yes, creating a free account allows you to apply for jobs, track applications, and save jobs for later.

Q. How do I upload my resume?

Go to your profile/dashboard and click "Upload Resume." Supported formats include PDF and DOCX.

Q. Can I edit or update my profile?

Yes, log in to your dashboard and click "Edit Profile" to update your details anytime.

Q. How do employers post jobs?

Employers can log in, go to the "Post Job" section, fill in job details, and publish the listing instantly.

Q. Is using JobPortal free for job seekers?

Yes, job seekers can search and apply for jobs free of charge.

Q. How can I track the jobs I applied for?

Go to your dashboard and check the "Jobs You Have Applied" section for a list of your applications.

Q. I forgot my password, how do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

Q. How can I contact JobPortal support?

Visit the "Contact" page and fill in the support form, or email us at support@jobportal.com.
