Help & FAQ for Recruiters

Q. How do I post a job?

Log in to your recruiter account, go to the "Post Job" section, fill in the job details, and click publish.

Q. Do I need an account to post jobs?

Yes, you must create a free employer account to post jobs and manage applications.

Q. Can I edit or update a job after posting?

Yes, go to your dashboard, select the job you posted, and click "Edit" to make changes anytime.

Q. How do I view applicants for my job?

Navigate to your dashboard and click on the job posting. Youâ€™ll see a list of applicants with their resumes.

Q. Can I contact candidates directly?

Yes, you can view applicant details and contact them directly via the email or phone number provided in their profile/resume.

Q. Is posting jobs free?

JobPortal allows free basic postings. For premium visibility, paid options may be available.

Q. How do I delete a job posting?

Go to your dashboard, find the job you want to remove, and click "Delete".

Q. Can multiple recruiters manage one company account?

Yes, company accounts can have multiple recruiters added for collaboration.

Q. How do I reset my recruiter password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, and follow the email instructions to reset your password.

Q. How can I get support for posting jobs?

Visit the "Contact" page or email us at recruiter-support@jobportal.com for assistance.
